## Purpose

This index catalogues Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) reporting artifacts for AMPEL360 programs, providing stakeholders with transparent access to sustainability performance data.

## ESG Framework

### Environmental (E)
**Focus Areas:**
- Carbon emissions (manufacturing, operations, lifecycle)
- Material circularity and waste reduction
- Energy efficiency and renewable energy adoption
- Water usage and conservation
- Biodiversity and ecosystem impact

### Social (S)
**Focus Areas:**
- Workforce safety and wellbeing
- Diversity, equity, and inclusion
- Community engagement and impact
- Supply chain labor practices
- Stakeholder consultation

### Governance (G)
**Focus Areas:**
- Sustainability governance structure
- Ethical business practices
- Transparency and reporting
- Compliance with environmental regulations
- Risk management for climate and social issues
